# Refunds and Refund Policy

## Understanding Refunds

When a booking is cancelled or circumstances change, Courtify handles refunds through a fair, transparent policy. This guide explains when refunds are available, how they're processed, and what to expect.

## Refund Eligibility

### Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a refund in these situations:

**Player-Initiated Cancellation**:
- You cancel a confirmed booking
- Timing affects refund percentage (see refund timeline below)

**Booking Rejection**:
- Court owner rejects your booking request
- No refund needed (you weren't charged yet, only pre-approval)

**Court Owner Cancellation**:
- Court owner cancels for legitimate reason
- Safety issue or emergency closure
- You receive full refund

**Payment Error**:
- You were charged twice for same booking
- Wrong amount was charged
- Unauthorized charge on your account
- You receive full refund plus correction

**Legitimate Dispute**:
- Court doesn't match description
- Safety hazard or maintenance issues
- Unfair cancellation by owner
- Case-by-case review by Courtify

### Not Eligible for Refund

These situations typically do not qualify for refund:

**No-Show**:
- You booked but didn't arrive
- Deposit is forfeited
- Court was reserved for you

**Very Late Cancellation**:
- Within 2-4 hours of booking time (per policy)
- Court couldn't rebook in time
- Deposit held as compensation

**Change of Mind**:
- After significant advance notice passed
- Personal circumstances changed
- Court held reservation for you

**Court Rules Violation**:
- You violated court's rules
- Disruptive behavior or damage
- Forfeited deposit

**Breach of Terms**:
- Violating Courtify terms of service
- Fraudulent booking
- Prohibited activity

## Refund Timeline

Refund amount depends on **when you cancel** relative to booking time:

### Full Refund (100%)

**Timing**: Cancel 24 hours or more before booking
- Example: Booking at 5:00 PM Friday → Cancel by 5:00 PM Thursday
- You receive full advance payment back
- No penalty or fees
- Refund processed within 3-7 business days
- Slot becomes available for others

**When Available**: 
- Soon after booking (if you change your mind)
- Day(s) before your booking
- Even day of, if 24+ hours remain

### Partial Refund (50%)

**Timing**: Cancel 12-24 hours before booking
- Example: Booking at 5:00 PM Friday → Cancel 5:00 PM Thursday to 5:00 PM Saturday morning
- You receive 50% of advance payment back
- 50% kept as cancellation fee
- Courts compensated for lost opportunity
- Refund processed within 3-7 business days

**When Available**:
- When shorter cancellation notice given
- Recognized as less ideal for court owner
- Partially refundable but with fee

### No Refund (0%)

**Timing**: Cancel within 12 hours of booking
- Example: Booking at 5:00 PM Friday → Cancel after 5:00 AM Friday
- Advance payment is forfeited
- You keep no money back
- Court fully compensated
- Booking slot not available for others

**When Applied**:
- Last-minute cancellations
- Very short notice
- Court cannot rebook slot
- Compensation for lost revenue

**Note**: Court-specific policies may vary. Always check individual court's cancellation policy before booking.

## Refund Process

### Step 1: Initiate Cancellation
1. Go to your booking in dashboard
2. Click "Cancel Booking" button
3. Confirm you want to cancel
4. System calculates refund amount based on timing
5. Review refund amount (if applicable)
6. Click "Confirm Cancellation"

### Step 2: Cancellation Submitted
- Booking status changes to "Cancellation Requested"
- Court owner is notified immediately
- Cancellation reason recorded (if you provided one)
- System calculates refund percentage

### Step 3: Refund Processing
- Courtify approves the cancellation
- Refund amount calculated per policy
- Refund initiated to your payment method
- Status changes to "Cancelled"

### Step 4: Refund Timeline
- **Courtify Processing**: 1-2 business days to initiate refund
- **Bank Processing**: 3-5 business days for funds to appear
- **Total**: Up to 7 business days typical
- **Varies**: Some banks faster, some slower

### Step 5: Refund Receipt
- Email confirmation of refund
- Shows refund amount
- Transaction reference number
- Expected arrival in account
- Keep for your records

### Step 6: Verify Refund
- Check bank account/card statement
- Search by transaction reference if needed
- If not arrived after 7 days, contact support
- Provide transaction number for investigation

## Special Refund Scenarios

### Court Owner Cancels Your Booking

If court owner cancels after you've paid:
- Full refund automatically processed
- Happens immediately or within 24 hours
- Courtify apologizes for inconvenience
- You're encouraged to book elsewhere
- Full refund deposited to your card

### Payment Declined But Charged

If payment shows declined but you were charged:
1. Don't panic - this can happen with processing
2. Wait 24-48 hours to verify
3. Check if booking shows "Confirmed" or "Pending Payment"
4. Contact support if charge remains after 48 hours
5. Provide transaction details
6. Full refund issued plus correction

### Double Charge

If you're charged twice for same booking:
1. Verify this actually happened (check transaction details)
2. Contact Courtify support immediately
3. Provide both transaction numbers
4. Provide booking confirmation
5. Full refund of duplicate charge issued
6. Credited within 3-5 business days

### Partial Payment Issues

If deposit amount doesn't match booking cost:
1. Review booking calculation (rate × hours)
2. Verify deposit percentage matches policy
3. If error confirmed, contact support
4. Explain the discrepancy
5. Courtify investigates and corrects
6. Refund of overcharge issued if applicable

### Booking Never Confirmed

If booking was approved but payment failed:
- You weren't actually charged
- No refund needed
- Booking still "Approved" awaiting payment
- Try payment again if you want to proceed
- Or cancel without penalty

## Disputing a Refund

### If Refund Not Received

After 7 business days from cancellation:

1. **Check Status**:
   - Log into Courtify
   - View cancellation in history
   - Check status of refund processing

2. **Contact Support**:
   - Provide booking ID
   - Provide transaction number
   - Explain refund expected timeline
   - Ask for refund status update

3. **Provide Documentation**:
   - Screenshots of cancellation
   - Confirmation emails
   - Bank statement showing no refund
   - Transaction references

4. **Investigation**:
   - Courtify contacts Stripe/payment processor
   - Verifies refund was issued
   - Tracks refund progress
   - Provides timeline for arrival

5. **Resolution**:
   - If refund was issued: You receive in 1-2 days typically
   - If refund wasn't processed: Immediate reissue
   - If stuck: Manual investigation and resolution

### Contacting Your Bank

If refund still hasn't arrived:

1. **Call Your Bank**:
   - Inform them of expected refund
   - Provide refund transaction number from Courtify
   - Ask them to check status
   - They can trace the refund

2. **Ask Bank For**:
   - Confirmation refund was received
   - Timeline for it to appear in your account
   - Any holds or delays on their end
   - Reference number for their investigation

3. **Bank May Discover**:
   - Refund is in processing
   - Refund arrived but shows as pending
   - Refund was sent to different account
   - Technical issue delaying posting

## Refund for Disputed Bookings

### When to Dispute

Dispute a booking charge if:
- Court was significantly different from description
- Unsafe or unhygienic conditions
- Court was non-functional (nets missing, broken floor, etc.)
- Blatant misrepresentation of facilities
- Safety hazard that made playing impossible
- Severe maintenance or cleanliness issues

### How to Dispute

1. **Gather Evidence**:
   - Photos of actual court condition
   - Notes about specific problems
   - Dates and times of issues
   - Any communication with court owner

2. **Contact Court Owner First**:
   - Explain the issue clearly
   - Ask for goodwill refund or replacement booking
   - Many issues can be resolved directly
   - Give owner chance to make right

3. **If Not Resolved, Contact Courtify**:
   - Go to booking details
   - Click "Report Issue" or "Dispute Booking"
   - Explain situation in detail
   - Attach photos or evidence
   - Submit for Courtify review

4. **Courtify Investigates**:
   - Reviews your claim
   - Requests court owner's response
   - May ask for additional details
   - Examines court photos and reviews
   - Makes fairness determination

5. **Resolution**:
   - If dispute upheld: Refund issued (full or partial)
   - If dispute declined: Charge stands
   - Decision communicated with explanation
   - Refund processed if approved

## Best Practices for Refunds

### Avoid Unnecessary Refunds

To keep your deposit:
- ✅ Cancel with adequate notice (24+ hours)
- ✅ Only book when reasonably confident
- ✅ Use favorites to book recurring courts
- ✅ Plan ahead to avoid last-minute changes
- ✅ Consider your schedule carefully before booking

### Maximize Your Refund

If you must cancel:
- ✅ Cancel as early as possible
- ✅ The earlier the better
- ✅ Cancelling at 24+ hours boundary is ideal
- ✅ Avoid cancelling between 12-24 hour window (50% lost)
- ✅ Never cancel within 12 hours (entire deposit lost)

### Track Your Refund

After cancellation:
- ✅ Save confirmation email
- ✅ Note transaction number
- ✅ Write down expected refund date
- ✅ Monitor bank account
- ✅ Contact support if delayed beyond 7 days

## Refund FAQs

**Q: How long does refund take?**
A: Up to 7 business days. Courtify processes in 1-2 days, then bank takes 3-5 days.

**Q: Can I get refund if I no-show?**
A: No. If you don't arrive, deposit is forfeited.

**Q: What if court owner cancels?**
A: You get full refund immediately.

**Q: Can I cancel a completed booking?**
A: No. Once time has passed, booking is final.

**Q: Does court cancellation for maintenance qualify for refund?**
A: Yes, full refund. Court should have notified you of maintenance.

**Q: What if I paid extra for any reason?**
A: Contact support - refund of overcharge will be issued.

**Q: Can refund go to different payment method?**
A: No. Refund returns to original card/payment method used.

**Q: Is there a fee for refunds?**
A: No. Refund fee structure described above, but no additional processing fees.

Managing refunds fairly ensures trust and confidence in the Courtify platform. Understanding the policy helps you make informed booking decisions!
