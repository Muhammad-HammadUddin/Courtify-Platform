# Managing Player Bookings

## Court Owner Booking Management

As a court owner, managing player bookings is central to your business. Courtify provides tools to efficiently handle booking requests, communicate with players, track reservations, and maintain smooth operations. This guide covers all aspects of booking management from your owner perspective.

## Accessing Booking Management

In your owner dashboard, go to the "Bookings" or "Reservations" section. Here you'll see:
- All incoming booking requests (pending approval)
- Approved bookings with confirmed dates/times
- Completed bookings (history)
- Cancelled bookings with reasons
- Calendar view of all reservations

## Understanding Booking Workflow

When a player books your court, the booking goes through several stages:

### Stage 1: Booking Request (Pending)
- Player submits booking request
- You receive notification
- Court slot is temporarily held
- Player hasn't paid yet
- You must approve or reject

### Stage 2: Approval Decision
- You review the request
- Click "Approve" to accept
- Click "Reject" if you can't accommodate
- Player notified of your decision
- Notification arrives via email/app

### Stage 3: Payment Processing
After approval:
- Player receives payment request
- Player pays advance deposit
- Courtify confirms payment
- Booking becomes "Confirmed"
- You're notified of confirmation

### Stage 4: Booking Confirmed
- Player has paid advance
- Booking is secured
- Slot is no longer available to others
- Player will arrive at scheduled time
- Player owes any remaining balance in person

### Stage 5: Completion
- After booking time passes
- Booking marked as "Completed"
- Booking moved to history
- Player can leave review and rating
- Transaction is archived

## Managing Pending Bookings

Pending bookings need your immediate attention - players are waiting for your response.

### Viewing Pending Requests
In your dashboard, filter to show only "Pending" bookings. Each shows:
- Player name and profile
- Requested date and time
- Duration (hours)
- Expected court fee
- Booking time (how long they've been waiting)
- Special requests or notes (if any)

### Reviewing a Booking Request
Click on a pending booking to see:
- Complete player profile (name, rating history)
- Detailed booking information
- Your court's availability at that time
- Any conflicts with other bookings
- Player's contact information
- Previous bookings history with you

### Approving Bookings

To approve a booking:

1. **Review the Request**: Verify date, time, and player
2. **Check Availability**: Confirm no conflicts exist
3. **Consider Your Schedule**: Ensure you can accommodate
4. **Click Approve**: Booking moves to approved status
5. **Confirmation Sent**: Player receives approval notification
6. **Player Pays**: Player then completes payment

**After Approval**:
- Player can now see "Pay Now" option
- You see "Awaiting Payment" status
- Once paid, booking confirms automatically
- You're notified of payment confirmation

### Rejecting Bookings

If you cannot accept a booking:

1. **Click Reject Button**: Access rejection option
2. **Provide Reason** (Optional): Helps player understand
   - Court maintenance scheduled
   - Double booking error
   - Staff unavailable
   - Other specific reason
3. **Confirm Rejection**: Booking rejected
4. **Notification Sent**: Player notified immediately
5. **Slot Reopens**: Available for other players to book

**Tips for Rejection**:
- Be professional and courteous
- Provide reason if possible
- Suggest alternative times if available
- Don't reject too frequently (damages reputation)

## Communicating with Players

### Sending Messages
From any booking, you can contact the player:
- Click "Contact Player" or message icon
- Compose your message
- Send through Courtify platform
- Message recorded for reference

**When to Message**:
- Clarify booking details
- Request additional information
- Provide special instructions
- Confirm arrival details
- Address concerns or questions

### Professional Communication
- Respond within 24 hours
- Use professional tone
- Be helpful and friendly
- Keep messages concise
- Avoid demanding or rude language

## Confirmed Bookings

Once a player has paid, the booking is confirmed.

### What Happens Next
- You know player is definitely coming
- Slot is secured (no other player can book)
- Prepare your court for their arrival
- Any remaining balance will be collected in person
- Player should arrive on time

### Player Responsibilities
- Arrive on time or slightly early
- Bring valid ID if needed
- Pay remaining balance (cash usually)
- Follow court rules
- Leave court in good condition

### Your Responsibilities
- Have court ready and clean
- Greet player professionally
- Ensure court meets description
- Collect remaining payment if applicable
- Ensure enjoyable experience

## Cancelled Bookings

Cancellations happen for various reasons. Handle them professionally.

### Player-Initiated Cancellations
When a player cancels their booking:
- Notification appears in your dashboard
- Reason for cancellation provided (if given)
- Slot becomes available for other players
- Player's refund is handled by Courtify
- Transaction marked as cancelled in history

### Managing Cancellation Impact
- Note if one player cancels frequently (potential unreliable customer)
- Your court slot is now available to re-book
- Search engine may update availability
- Consider if you want to promote the now-available slot

### Owner-Initiated Cancellations
In rare cases, you may need to cancel:

1. **Legitimate Reasons Only**:
   - Emergency facility closure
   - Safety hazard
   - Unexpected major maintenance
   - Confirmed double-booking error

2. **Cancellation Process**:
   - Go to confirmed booking
   - Select "Cancel Booking" (if available)
   - Provide explanation
   - Confirm cancellation
   - Player notified immediately

3. **Consequences**:
   - Player receives full refund
   - Player relationship potentially damaged
   - Your cancellation rate visible to Courtify
   - Excessive cancellations can lead to penalties

**Important**: Cancel only when absolutely necessary.

## Monitoring Your Bookings

### Booking Statistics
Track important metrics:
- **Total Bookings**: How many bookings overall
- **Pending Bookings**: How many await your approval
- **Approval Rate**: What percentage you approve vs. reject
- **Cancellation Rate**: How often bookings are cancelled
- **Revenue**: Total earned from confirmed bookings

### Booking Trends
Identify patterns:
- Popular time slots (peak hours)
- Busy vs. slow days (weekday vs. weekend)
- Seasonal trends (time of year effects)
- Player preferences (duration, frequency)
- Pricing impact (if you adjust rates)

### Using Data
- Adjust hours based on demand
- Price strategically for peak times
- Plan maintenance during slow periods
- Market your court when demand is low
- Understand your customer base

## Handling Special Situations

### No-Shows (Player Doesn't Arrive)
If player doesn't show for booking:
- Note the time
- Document the absence
- Check if player has pattern of no-shows
- Don't charge again, but recognize issue
- Can address with player next time they book

### Payment Disputes
If player questions charges:
- Review booking confirmation
- Show calculation (rate Ã— hours = total)
- Explain advance paid vs. balance due
- Resolve professionally
- Contact support if unresolved

### Booking Errors or Conflicts
If same slot is accidentally double-booked:
- Identify which booking came first
- Approve the legitimate one
- Contact and apologize to other player
- Offer alternative time or refund
- Prevent future errors

### Last-Minute Changes
If player requests time change just before booking:
- If new time is available: Re-book if possible
- If new time is booked: Suggest cancellation and re-booking
- Help player find suitable alternative
- Be flexible but protect other bookings

## Managing High Booking Volume

If you're very popular and fully booked:
- Consider raising prices
- Add additional court hours if possible
- Expand facilities if feasible
- Create waiting lists
- Monitor for quality issues

If slots are going unfilled:
- Consider lowering prices
- Promote your court more
- Improve court description/photos
- Improve customer service
- Identify and address issues

Effective booking management is essential to running a successful court facility on Courtify. Stay organized, communicate clearly, and focus on player satisfaction for best results!
